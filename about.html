About Graphic Design

Graphic Design waa farsamo lagu abuuro visuals qurux badan sida logos, posters iyo websites. Waxa uu isku daraa creativity + technology.

🎨 What is Graphic Design?

Waa farshaxan casri ah oo lagu sameeyo design muuqaal leh si loo gudbiyo fariin.

🛠 Tools Used

Photoshop, Illustrator, Canva iyo tools kale oo digital design ah ayaa la isticmaalaa.

🚀 Why it Matters

Waxay ka caawisaa business-yada inay helaan brand xoog leh iyo macaamiil badan.
